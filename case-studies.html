Case Study: Building Website Professional Services

Overview

Created and managed Website Professional Services from scratch as a solo contributor, building end-to-end processes, pricing, and tracking systems.

Challenge

No structured or monetized approach existed, leading to inefficiencies, lack of visibility, and missed revenue.

Approach

Market research to benchmark competitors’ tiers and pricing. Designed tiered packages. Built workflows/SOPs across Confluence, Zendesk, and SharePoint. Developed Excel tracking system. Created pitch deck advocating expansion. Applied AI diagnostics to solve process/technical issues.

Impact

Established a replicable, revenue-generating service, improved workflows and visibility, and demonstrated ability to convert stakeholder needs into strategies.

Case Study: AI-Assisted Problem Solving Across Roles

Overview

Applied AI tools across multiple roles to resolve technical/process challenges and improve workflows.

Challenge

Teams faced recurring errors (CRM DKIM failures, Zendesk triggers, Jira misuse, SaaS content bottlenecks) with engineering escalations deprioritized.

Approach

AI diagnostics on email headers uncovered DKIM mismatches. AI troubleshooting identified Zendesk trigger workarounds. Flagged Jira misuse with new classification guidelines.

Impact

Produced actionable technical recommendations, prevented client communication breakdowns, and demonstrated ability to bridge business, technical, and AI tools.

Case Study: Stakeholder Communication & Business Case Development

Overview

Identified the need to scale Website Professional Services, developed a strategic pitch deck, and reframed the offering as a revenue-generating business line.

Challenge

Company lacked scalability and monetization strategy; service worked for one person but required leadership buy-in for growth.

Approach

Market research and competitor benchmarking. Designed tiered service packages. Created mockups and process diagrams for scalability. Structured pitch deck highlighting revenue, resourcing, risk mitigation. Presented tailored findings to leadership.

Impact

Positioned services as a scalable revenue-generating line, demonstrated strategic communication skills, and showcased business case development ability.
